Holistic Treatment for Back Pain

Back pain is one of the most common complaints in modern society. Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) offers proven, root-cause-oriented treatment methods – gentle, effective and without side effects.

Common Types of Back Pain

  • • Lower back pain (Lumbago / LBP)
  • • Sciatica / Pain radiating to the leg
  • • Disc herniation / Disc prolapse
  • • Muscle tension & myofascial tightness
  • • Facet joint blocks / spinal joint restriction
  • • Neck & shoulder pain (Cervical spine)
  • • Acute lumbago (“back locked up”)
  • • Pain from prolonged sitting or work posture

Why Choose TCM for Back Pain?

Traditional Chinese Medicine does not only treat symptoms – it addresses the root cause of pain.

  • ✓ Releases deep muscle tension
  • ✓ Regulates the flow of Qi along the spine
  • ✓ Improves blood circulation & tissue regeneration
  • ✓ Reduces inflammation & nerve irritation
  • ✓ Holistic approach instead of short-term symptom relief

Common Causes from a TCM Perspective

  • ✓ Qi and Blood stagnation (“blocked energy”, pulling or stabbing pain)
  • ✓ Cold & Dampness in the lower back (common in winter or with AC exposure)
  • ✓ Kidney deficiency (typical in chronic lower back pain)
  • ✓ Muscle & tendon exhaustion (long sitting / physical overwork)
  • ✓ Blockage of meridians along the spine

A precise diagnosis is always individual – based on pain location, intensity, and accompanying symptoms.
